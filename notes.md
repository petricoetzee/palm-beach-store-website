# Palm Beach Store Website Mockup Notes

## What this is

A simple one-page website mockup for The Palm Beach Store Waiheke.

Soft pitch:

> I mocked up what a tiny one-page site could look like using what is already public online. It gives people the essentials fast: hours, location, what you sell, contact details, social links and directions.

## Public facts used

Sources checked included Yheke, Facebook/Instagram search snippets, AucklandNZ, eRide Waiheke, Wanderlog and Waiheke Gulf News.

Public facts found:

- Name: The Palm Beach Store / The Palm Beach Store Waiheke.
- Address: Shop 2, 39 Palm Road, Palm Beach, Waiheke Island 1081.
- Phone: +64 9 372 8149.
- Email listed by Yheke: palmbeachstorewaiheke@gmail.com.
- Website: Not Available on Yheke.
- Facebook: facebook.com/pbswaiheke.
- Instagram: instagram.com/palmbeachstorewaiheke.
- Public positioning/social snippet: local beachside store open daily for excellent coffee and “world-famous” toasties.
- Yheke overview says they sell hot coffees, toasties, hand-scooped ice creams, fruit smoothies, baked goods, snacks, toiletries, groceries, drinks and beach essentials.
- Yheke listed hours: mostly 7am–7pm, with Wednesday 8am–7pm.
- Some other public sources show different hours, so hours must be confirmed.
- Parking out front and wheelchair access are listed publicly.
- Waiheke Gulf News archives mention the store as iconic and reopening after flood/refurbishment in 2017.

## Details to verify before publishing

- Exact preferred business name and capitalization.
- Current hours.
- Current menu items and prices.
- Whether the email is actively monitored.
- Whether they prefer phone, email, Facebook or Instagram contact.
- Whether public photos may be used, or whether they would supply better images.
- Whether “world-famous toasties” is a phrase they still like.
- Whether they want one-page only, or pages for menu / beach info.

## Possible paid package

Keep scope tight:

- One-page mobile-first website
- Hours/location/contact/socials
- Menu highlights, not full live menu
- Beach-day positioning
- Map/directions buttons
- One revision round
- Basic noindex/staging preview before launch

Possible price range:

- Friendly/local pilot: $350–$600
- Standard one-page local site: $750–$1,200
- Optional yearly care: $150–$300/year for small text/hour changes, hosting/domain separate if needed

## Presentation wording

Avoid:

> You need a website.

Prefer:

> I noticed most of the useful info is scattered across Facebook, Instagram and directory listings, so I mocked up a tiny one-page version that puts it all in one clean place. Just a concept, not a finished site.
